How We Started

MediPocket Was Founded In California, USA

Our story is a testament to the power of innovation and unwavering commitment. It all started with one doctor and one immigrant from India Dr. Priyanka Mathur, who witnessed the global struggle to access top-tier healthcare during critical and complex illnesses.


This experience ignited the vision to transform healthcare accessibility by Bridging the Global Gap with advanced medical care in the USA. Our founder, deeply passionate about making a difference, embarked on this journey. Today, we proudly stand as a trusted force, infusing innovation, compassion, and excellence into every facet of our mission.


Our story epitomizes perseverance, a shared vision, and the relentless determination to create a world where Premium Healthcare Transcends Borders

From Our Founder

Dr. Priyanka Mathur

Our founder, witnessed the critical disparity in healthcare accessibility in India, where the WHO-recommended doctor-patient ratio is 1:1000, yet the reality stands at 1:11,580. India grapples with 70 million cancer patients, 77 million diabetics, 90 million rare diseases, and similarly alarming figures for heart diseases, mental health, and infertility.Dr. Mathur, a dedicated Indian-born physician, has felt the patients’ struggle firsthand. In response, she created Cross-Border Care, a pioneering platform that bridges this specialty care gap by seamlessly connecting patients in India to the world’s leading hospitals in the USA. Dr. Priyanka Mathur’s background is enriched with international healthcare experience in India, Russia, and the USA. Her passion lies at the intersection of AI and healthcare, a testament to her commitment to innovative solutions in the medical field. Our team comprises passionate individuals from diverse backgrounds—doctors, engineers, designers, and believers—united by a shared vision. We are dedicated to using the science of artificial intelligence to make top-quality healthcare accessible to patients worldwide. Our journey is defined by a resolute mission: to erase borders and provide the best care to those in need, regardless of location
